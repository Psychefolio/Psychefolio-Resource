MODULE 03
Coming Back to the Present

Grounding techniques interrupt the nervous system's spiral. They work by anchoring you to now — the present moment, which is safe. Here are three practices to learn and keep.

THE 5-4-3-2-1 GROUNDING METHOD
5 things you can see right now
4 things you can physically feel (floor, air, fabric)
3 things you can hear
2 things you can smell
1 thing you can taste
DO IT NOW — THEN WRITE HOW YOUR BODY FEELS AFTER
BOX BREATHING — 4 COUNTS EACH SIDE
Inhale for 4 counts
Hold for 4 counts
Exhale for 4 counts
Hold for 4 counts — repeat 4 times
