MODULE 04
Scripts That Actually Work

Narcissists are skilled at turning your words against you. These scripts are short, calm, and unargumentable. You don't need to justify a boundary — you just need to state it.

The Grey Rock response — when they push for a reaction:
"I hear you. I'm not going to discuss this further."
PRACTISE: Write what you wish you had said in a recent situation
The boundary statement — when they cross a line:
"When you [specific behaviour], I feel [impact]. I need [clear boundary]. If it continues, I will [consequence]."
NOW WRITE YOUR OWN VERSION FOR A REAL SITUATION
The exit script — when you need to leave a conversation:
"I'm not able to continue this conversation right now. I'll come back to it when things are calmer."
