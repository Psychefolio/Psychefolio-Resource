01 —— Grandiosity & Entitlement 0 / 8
Acts as if rules don't apply to them — skips queues, ignores policies, expects exceptions
Frequently boasts about achievements, status, wealth, or connections
Expects constant praise and admiration without reciprocating
Believes they are "special" and should only associate with equally special people
Exaggerates their role in events or takes credit for other people's work
Name-drops, status-drops, or constantly references their "important" connections
Gets visibly irritated when they are not the centre of attention
Speaks about their future in grandiose terms — power, fame, wealth — with no self-awareness
02 —— Lack of Empathy 0 / 7
Shows little or no genuine interest in your feelings, struggles, or inner life
Turns your emotional conversations back around to themselves within minutes
Dismisses your pain with "you're too sensitive" or "you're overreacting"
Uses your vulnerabilities or personal confessions against you later
Appears cold or unmoved when others are visibly upset or in crisis
Only shows warmth or care when there is something to gain from it
Cannot or will not acknowledge how their behaviour affects other people
03 —— Manipulation & Control 0 / 8
Makes you question your own memory or perception of events (gaslighting)
Uses guilt, silent treatment, or withdrawal of affection to punish or control you
Triangulates — uses a third person to make you feel jealous, insecure, or in competition
Love-bombed at the start — overwhelming affection and flattery — then withdrew it suddenly
Shifts the goalposts — what was acceptable yesterday is suddenly wrong today
Controls your access to people, money, or information to keep you dependent
Plays the victim when confronted, turning the situation so you end up apologising
Uses charm strategically — entirely different person in public vs in private
04 —— Rage, Criticism & Reactions 0 / 7
Reacts to mild criticism or perceived slights with extreme anger or contempt (narcissistic rage)
Cannot accept being wrong — always has a reason, excuse, or someone else to blame
Holds grudges and pursues revenge — even over very minor perceived wrongs
Criticises you freely but cannot handle any criticism directed back at them
Stonewalls or gives silent treatment as punishment rather than to process emotions
Smears your reputation to others when you threaten their image or try to leave
Their mood is unpredictable — you walk on eggshells, never knowing what will trigger them
05 —— How They Make You Feel 0 / 5
You feel confused, exhausted, or "crazy" after interactions with them
You find yourself constantly apologising even when you've done nothing wrong
Your self-esteem has noticeably declined since this person entered your life
You feel isolated from friends or family — either by their actions or your own shame
You feel like you can never do enough, be enough, or win — no matter what you try
06 —— Relationships & Social Patterns 0 / 5
Has a trail of burned bridges — ex-friends and partners who "turned on them for no reason"
People are either all-good (idealised) or all-bad (devalued) — no nuance in between
Surrounds themselves with people who agree with them or reflect their image back (narcissistic supply)
Uses people for status, connections, or attention and discards them when no longer useful
Envies others' success deeply, while simultaneously believing others are envious of them
